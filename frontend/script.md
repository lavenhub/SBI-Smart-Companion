# 🚀 SBI Smart Companion – Live Demo Script

*A step-by-step guide for presenting to the judges. Follow the "Action" steps, read the "Script" out loud, and point out the "Result" on the screen.*

## 🎯 Part 1: The Command Palette (Speed & Navigation)

🗣️ **Script:** "Welcome to the SBI Smart Companion – a completely frictionless digital banking experience. Let's start with navigation. Instead of digging through menus, we built a lightning-fast Command Palette."

🔹 **Action:** Press `Cmd + K` (or `Ctrl + K`).
🗣️ **Script:** "I just hit Command K to open search."

🔹 **Action:** Type `Smart Vault` and hit `Enter`.
👀 **Result:** The app instantly routes to the Smart Vault page.
🗣️ **Script:** "It instantly routes us to any page. But it’s also contextual."

🔹 **Action:** Press `Cmd + K`, type `Scan & Pay QR`, and hit `Enter`.
👀 **Result:** The app routes to the UPI page AND auto-opens the Pay modal.
🗣️ **Script:** "Searching 'Scan & Pay' doesn't just open the UPI page, it opens the exact payment wizard we need. Zero friction."

## 🤖 Part 3: The Star of the Show (AI Voice Banking Agent)

🗣️ **Script:** "But what if you don't want to type at all? Let's use the new AI Voice Banking Agent."

### A. Voice Receipt Generation
🔹 **Action:** Click the **Floating Mic Button**.
🗣️ **Script:** "Create a receipt of 25,000 rupees for bought a fridge."
👀 **Result:** The agent extracts the amount (₹25k), note, and category. It auto-saves and jumps directly to the Vault.
🗣️ **Script:** "Using Natural Language Processing, it extracts the exact amount, context, and automatically routes the generated receipt to the Vault."

### B. Conversational Database Search
🔹 **Action:** Click the **Floating Mic Button**.
🗣️ **Script:** "How much did I spend on bills?"
👀 **Result:** The panel shows a 📊 Database Search card summing up the ₹3,000 electricity bill.
🗣️ **Script:** "It also acts as a conversational database. Ask it to query your vault, and it instantly calculates your spending."

### C. AI Financial Predictor
🔹 **Action:** Click the **Floating Mic Button**.
🗣️ **Script:** "Can I afford to buy a Macbook?"
👀 **Result:** The panel shows a 📈 Financial Projection card deducting EMIs from the balance and approving the purchase.
🗣️ **Script:** "It’s also a Financial Advisor. It analyzes your current balance against projected purchases and upcoming EMIs to give you a personalized affordability verdict."

### D. Voice Biometrics (High Security)
🔹 **Action:** Click the **Floating Mic Button**.
🗣️ **Script:** "Increase my UPI limit."
👀 **Result:** The agent turns RED (🔐 Biometric Auth) and asks for a passphrase.
🗣️ **Script:** *(Wait for the red animation)* "My voice is my password."
👀 **Result:** The agent verifies the voice and opens the limit modal on the UPI page.
🗣️ **Script:** "For high-security actions, the agent triggers Voice Biometrics. Your voice becomes your ultimate password."
